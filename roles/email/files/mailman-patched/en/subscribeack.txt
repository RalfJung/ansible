Normally, Mailman will remind you of your %(host_name)s mailing list

prefer.  This reminder will also include instructions on how to
unsubscribe or change your account options.  There is also a button on
your options page that will email your current password to you.

Data protection:
To send emails to you, we are storing your email address. It is only
visible to list owners. You can delete it any time at %(optionsurl)s.
Furthermore, emails you are sending to the list are being archived --
including the sender and all destination email adresses of the email.
This archive is available publicly on the internet in the case of
public lists; otherwise it is available to current and future list
members.
